🏫 ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Building Tomorrow's Leaders
Through Excellence in Education

Founded in 1974, Kalinabiri Secondary School stands as Uganda's most prestigious educational institution, combining over 50 years of academic excellence with forward-thinking innovation. Our commitment to holistic development — blending rigorous academics, character formation, and extracurricular mastery — has produced generations of leaders, scholars, and entrepreneurs who shape Uganda's future.

At Kalinabiri, we believe every student possesses unique potential. Our world-class faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and nurturing environment work together to unlock that potential, preparing young minds to compete and lead on the global stage.
