📋 ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Entry Requirements

We welcome applications from students who meet our academic and character standards. Choose the program that suits your situation.

🏠

Boarding Students

• Completion of PLE (P.7) with a pass grade
• Birth Certificate (original & copy)
• PLE Result Slip (original)
• Health Form completed by a registered medical practitioner
• 4 passport-sized photos
• Guardian's National ID copy
• Proof of vaccination/immunization
• Completed boarding agreement form

🚶

Day Students

• Completion of PLE (P.7) with a pass grade
• Birth Certificate (original & copy)
• PLE Result Slip (original)
• Health Form from a registered clinic/hospital
• 4 passport-sized photos
• Guardian's National ID copy
• Proof of residential address within Kampala/Ntinda area

🔄

Re-joining Students

• Previous school report card (last received)
• Transfer/Leave Letter from previous school
• Discipline record from previous headteacher
• Birth Certificate copy
• Guardian's National ID copy
• Completed re-application form
• Interview with Dean of Students may be required
