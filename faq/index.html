Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Kalinabiri SS

What are the admission requirements?

+

S.1 Admission: PLE Result Slip with at least 4 passes, Birth Certificate, Guardian's National ID, passport photos (6), and medical form. Interview may be required.

S.5 Admission: UCE Result Slip with at least 8 passes including English and Math, Birth Certificate, previous school Transfer Letter, and good conduct certificate.

How much are school fees?

+

Tuition ranges from UGX 1,200,000 to UGX 2,500,000 per term depending on the program and level:

  • Day Students: UGX 1,200,000 - 1,500,000 per term
  • Boarding Students: UGX 2,000,000 - 2,500,000 per term
  • A-Level Science: UGX 1,800,000 - 2,200,000 per term

Fees cover tuition, meals (boarders), accommodation, sports, and access to e-learning platform. Additional charges apply for uniforms, transport, and examination fees.

Is boarding available?

+

Yes! Full boarding facilities are available for S.1 to S.6 students. Our dormitories accommodate 200+ students with:

  • Spacious, well-ventilated rooms (2-4 students per room)
  • 24/7 supervision by trained house parents
  • Nutritious meals (breakfast, lunch, supper, and evening snack)
  • Evening study sessions with academic support
  • Weekend recreational activities

What are the term dates?

+

Our academic year runs in three terms:

  • Term I: February to April (14 weeks)
  • Term II: May to August (14 weeks)
  • Term III: August to November/December (14 weeks)

Exact dates are published in the school calendar and on the parent portal at the beginning of each year.

How do I apply?

+

You can apply through any of these methods:

  1. Online: Complete the application form on our website or student portal
  2. In-Person: Visit the Admissions Office on campus during working hours (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm)
  3. Download: Get the admission form from our downloads page, fill it, and submit with required documents

For inquiries, call +256 700 123 456 or email admissions@kalinabiriss.ac.ug

What subjects are offered?

+

O-Level (S.1-S.4): 13 subjects including English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, Religious Education, Entrepreneurship, Computer, Fine Art, Music, and Physical Education.

A-Level (S.5-S.6) Combinations:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • MEG (Mathematics, Economics, Geography)
  • HAK (History, Art, Kiswahili)
  • DLM (Divinity, Literature, Music)

Is transportation available?

+

Yes, school buses serve Kampala and surrounding areas. Routes include:

  • Northern Route: Kireka, Bweyogerere, Seeta
  • Eastern Route: Ntinda, Naalyamfidho, Kulambiro
  • Southern Route: Bugolobi, Nakawa, Luzira
  • Western Route: Kisasi, Namugongo, Kira

Transport fees range from UGX 400,000 to UGX 800,000 per term depending on distance. Buses operate from 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM on school days.

What security measures are in place?

+

We prioritize student safety with comprehensive security measures:

  • 24/7 security personnel at all entry points
  • CCTV surveillance cameras throughout campus
  • Guarded main gate with visitor registration
  • School nurse on duty 24/7
  • Fire safety equipment and evacuation plans
  • Background-checked staff and service providers
  • Secure dormitory access with biometric systems
